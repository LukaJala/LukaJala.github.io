A few recipes

  • Water + lava forges stone — pour the sea into the crater and build land.
  • Lava kisses sand into glass. Explosions shatter it right back.
  • Fire loves oil, wood, and plants. Gunpowder loves fire enthusiastically.
  • Steam rises, cools, and rains back down. A whole water cycle.
  • Plants drink water to grow. Ice creeps across still ponds.
  • Acid eats nearly everything — but never glass, and never wall.
  • Spouts pour forever; the void devours forever. Plumbing awaits.

Secrets

  • The island keeps things buried. Dig around the seabed with the eraser.
  • The volcano is stable… until someone cracks the crater rim.
  • Every world is procedural — press R for a new island, forever.
